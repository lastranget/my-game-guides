Athena Asamiya — King of Fighters XI guide

Athena Asamiya — psychic idol and one of KOF’s premier zoners. A fast Psycho Ball, an invincible anti-air DM, crossups, a command throw, and a Leader super that does ~60%. This guide covers her KOF XI moves, every combo from the source FAQ, her game plan, and her full matchup chart.

How KOF XI works — controls & systemscontents ↑

Everything you need to play, beyond this character’s own moves. KOF XI is a 3-on-1 tag fighter (you pick a team of three + a Leader, but only one fights at a time).

Movement
Walk / dash / backstepFF · BB
Tap f,f (hold) to run; b,b to hop back (brief invincibility).
JumpsU tap = hop
Tap up = a short hop; press up = a full jump. There are also fast-hops and high jumps. Hops are the heart of KOF offence.
BlockB / DB
Hold back to block high, down-back to block low. You cannot block in the air in KOF.
Defence
Roll (Emergency Evade)
Roll forward through an attack (invincible on start-up, then vulnerable). Back-roll with b+AB.
Guard Cancel roll / blowback /
While blocking, spend 1 stock to roll out (AB) or to shove them off with the blowback (E).
Fallbreaker (recovery roll) landing
Tap AB just before you hit the ground to recover and avoid an OTG (not vs. hard-knockdown moves).
Guard Crush
Every block chips your guard gauge; let it empty and you’re Crushed — stunned and wide open. Stop blocking forever.
Offence
Throw / throw escapeF
Close f/b + C or D throws (unblockable); the same input the instant you’re grabbed escapes a ground throw.
Blowback (CD)
The E button — a knockdown blow that’s cancellable on many characters and great as a poke / anti-air.
Stun gauge
The red bar above your life; fill it and the opponent is STUNNED for a free combo.
Team — tags & shifts
Tag out (Multi-Shift) /
Swap to a teammate any time on the ground — but you can be hit out of it, so do it safe.
Quick Shift attacking
Cancel your own attack to tag a partner in airborne for a follow-up — costs 1 skill point. The basis of team combos.
Saving Shift when hit
When you’re being hit, tag a partner in to break the opponent’s pursuit — costs both skill points.
Skill gauge
The bar above the power stocks; auto-charges to 2 points. Pays for Super/Dream Cancels and Quick/Saving Shifts.
Meter, cancels & the Leader
Power stocks & Desperation Moves
Attacking fills the power gauge into stocks (up to 5). A DM (super) costs 1 stock.
Leader & LDM
You pick one Leader (green gauge). Only the Leader can do the Leader DM (2 stocks) and Dream Cancels.
Super Cancel
Cancel a special into a DM — needs 1 skill point + 1 stock.
Dream Cancel
Cancel a DM into the Leader’s LDM — Leader only, 1 skill point + 3 stocks. The big-damage finisher.
Judgement (time-over)
If time runs out, the player who dealt more / landed an LDM wins (the timer colour shows who’s ahead).

Strengths & weaknessescontents ↑

Athena · Zoner / mix-up

Strengths

  • Elite zoning — Psycho Ball is fast with quick recovery.
  • A near-guaranteed anti-air DM in Shining Crystal Bit (invincible start-up).
  • A ~60% Leader super — Super Phoenix Infinity.
  • Fast and agile, with strong air mobility and crossups (Phoenix Bomb / Arrow).
  • Has a command throw and an air throw, and can reflect projectiles.

Weaknesses

  • Psycho Sword (her DP) is slow / low-priority — a poor ground anti-air or wake-up.
  • Many of her combos are corner-only.
  • Lost cancellability on some normals vs older KOF (e.g. cr.D).
  • Struggles against strong ground-priority rushdown (Kim especially).

Strategy, neutral & mix-upscontents ↑

Neutral & zoning

Keep moving + fireball
Great agility — never sit still; abuse Psycho Ball from range.
Anti-air
When they jump, Shining Crystal Bit or the LDM — both work even as you rise.
The fireball trap
Mid-screen Psycho Ball loop — if they roll: Link Kick / cr.C; if they jump: Crystal Bit / LDM; if close: st.C / cr.A.

Mix-ups & escapes

Teleport feint
Cancel cr.C into Psychic Teleport to cross up / confuse.
Retreat
back, back then air Phoenix Bomb to regain ground fast.
Corner escape
Use the triangle jump to leave the corner — don’t rely on Psycho Sword.
