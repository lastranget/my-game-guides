How to read these comboscontents ↑

Combos read left→right with a faint › between steps; italics are notes (Brake, T.O.P., land…). Difficulty is ●○○–●●●; core marks the staples.

Weak Punch — green (Y)
Hard Punch — blue (X)
Weak Kick — yellow (B)
Hard Kick — red (A)
any Punch
any Kick

Garou uses four buttons — A weak punch, B weak kick, C hard punch, D hard kick. AC = a Feint; CD = blowback / the T.O.P. attack. (Brake) = cancel a special’s recovery with A+B.

Motions are facing-relative. A named pill (e.g. The Hind) is a special move — hover for the input. Supers cost S.Power (1 stock); Potentials the P.Power (2 stocks). In your T.O.P. zone you also get the CD T.O.P. attack + a damage buff.

Combos — by use-casecontents ↑

Grouped by use-case with reference IDs. Specials are named pills (hover for input). Built from MGA’s Combo FAQ + the movelists + Garou’s Brake/Break & super-cancel rules.

Terry Bogard · All-round

Bread & butter
T-01BnB → Power Wave●○○ core
j. › › F+ XX › Power Wave
Jump-in, close C, the f+C command hit, cancel into Power Wave. Terry’s staple.
T-02BnB → Power Dunk●○○ core
j. › XX › Power Dunk
Jump-in, close D, into the Power Dunk (knockdown anti-air ender).
T-03BnB → Crack Shoot●○○
j. › XX › Crack Shoot
Crack Shoot ender — an overhead-ish flip kick, knocks down.
Pokes & low confirms
T-04Low confirm●○○
cr. › cr. XX › Power Wave
Two crouch B into the LP Power Wave — opens crouchers, safe poke string.
Brake / Break cancels
T-05Power Charge string●●○
j. › › F+ XX › Power Charge ×3
End in Power Charge and mash it up to three hits. Drop the f+C on tougher links.
T-06Power Dunk → Break → juggle●●● core
j. › › Power Dunk Break (A+B) › juggle Power Charge
Brake the Power Dunk’s first hit with A+B, then juggle — Terry’s signature damage.
T-07Feint pressure●●●
close › Burn Knuckle feint › then XX › Burn Knuckle
Close C, feint the Burn Knuckle (f+AC), then a jab links — a frame-trap into the real Knuckle.
Into a Super (S.Power)
T-08BnB → Power Geyser●●○ core
j. › › F+ XX › Power Geyser
Hit-confirm the chain into the Power Geyser super (S.Power).
T-09BnB → Buster Wolf●●○
j. › › F+ XX › Buster Wolf
Same confirm into Buster Wolf — the famous rushing super.
Potential, T.O.P. & corner
T-10Confirm → Potential Buster Wolf●●○
cr. › cr. › XX › Buster Wolf (Potential)
With two power stocks, end in the Potential (P.Power) Buster Wolf for big damage.
T-11Break → Power Geyser●●●
j. › › Power Dunk Break › then Power Geyser
Brake the Dunk and super-cancel into Power Geyser.
T-12T.O.P. attack combo●●○
in T.O.P. j. › XX › Max Dunk T.O.P. attack
Inside your T.O.P. zone, CD is a powered T.O.P. attack you can cancel a chain into.
