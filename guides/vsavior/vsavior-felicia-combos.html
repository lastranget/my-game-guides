How to read this guidecontents ↑

Combos read left→right with a faint › between steps; italics are notes. Inputs use coloured buttons matching your AYN Thor and short, facing-relative motion tokens. Difficulty is ●○○–●●●.

Light Punch — green (Y)
Medium Punch — grey
Hard Punch — blue (X)
Light Kick — yellow (B)
Medium Kick — grey
Hard Kick — red (A)
any Punch
any Kick

Vampire Savior is a six-button fighter — three punches (LP/MP/HP) and three kicks (LK/MK/HK). Light/hard sit on your coloured Thor face buttons; the mediums go on the grey shoulders. PP/KK = the ES (Especial) version of a move.

Motions are facing-relative — F = toward the opponent, B = away. A named pill (e.g. Shadow Blade) is a move — hover for the input.

Felicia · Combo rushdown

Bread & butter — chains
FE-01Stand chain●○○ 6h core
Felicia’s full six-hit zig-zag chain — she’s one of the best chain characters.
FE-02Chain → Rolling Buckler●●○ core
XX › Rolling Buckler → Uppercut (P)

XX › Cat Spike

Into an EX super
FE-05Chain → Dancing Flash●●○ core
XX › Dancing Flash
Cancel the chain into the rolling super.
FE-06Chain → Please Help Me●●○
XX › Please Help Me
The cat-swarm super — LK+MK straight, MK+HK jumping, LK+HK diving.
Pursuit / OTG & corner
FE-07Knockdown → Toy Touch●●○
Delta Kick knockdown › then Toy Touch OTG
After a Delta Kick knockdown, Toy Touch (d,d+P) keeps the OTG pressure going.
FE-08Corner Rolling loop●●●
corner XX › Rolling Buckler Sliding Kick (K) › OTG cr. › repeat
In the corner the Sliding-Kick ending of the Buckler lets you relink and loop.
Dark Force & resets
FE-09Cat Helper loop●●●
Cat Helper Dark Force › then Kitty follows up
Kitty attacks after each of your hits — set up big extended combos.
