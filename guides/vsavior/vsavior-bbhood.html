How to read this guidecontents ↑

Inputs use coloured buttons matching your AYN Thor and short, facing-relative motion tokens. Difficulty is ●○○–●●●.

Light Punch — green (Y)
Medium Punch — grey
Hard Punch — blue (X)
Light Kick — yellow (B)
Medium Kick — grey
Hard Kick — red (A)
any Punch
any Kick

Vampire Savior is a six-button fighter — three punches (LP/MP/HP) and three kicks (LK/MK/HK). Light/hard sit on your coloured Thor face buttons; the mediums go on the grey shoulders. PP/KK = the ES (Especial) version of a move.

Motions are facing-relative — F = toward the opponent, B = away. A named pill (e.g. Shadow Blade) is a move — hover for the input.

How Vampire Savior workscontents ↑

Everything you need to play, beyond this character’s own moves. VS is a fast 1-on-1 fighter with recoverable health and no rounds.

Movement
Dash / backstepFF / BB
Tap toward / away twice. Many characters can air-dash or double-jump too.
Jump / high jumpDU
Tap up to jump; tap down then up for a high jump. Several cast members have a second air jump.
Defence
Guard / air-guardB / DB
Hold back (high) or down-back (low); you can air-guard in VS.
Advancing Guard (tech)blocking
Tap P/K while blocking to push the attacker away.
Guard Cancel
While blocking, do your Guard-Cancel special to interrupt pressure (costs Chi).
Tech / soften throws
Throw the instant you’re thrown (or tap b/f + a hard button) to soften it.
Offence
Chain combos
Cancel attacks weak → strong, zig-zagging punches and kicks: LP → LK → MP → MK → HP → HK. Tap the next button on the hit-spark.
ThrowsF
Close b/f + MP/HP or MK/HK.
Pursuit (OTG)U
When the foe is down, u + P/K for an extra hit; do it with two buttons (ES) for more.
ES (Especial) moves
Do a special with two buttons (PP/KK) for a stronger EX version — costs one Chi level.
Chi, supers & the match
Chi (special stock)
Builds as you deal/​take damage (combos fill it fast). Spend it on ES moves, EX supers, Guard Cancels and Dark Force.
EX supers (Dramatic Moves)
Big named supers — most cost 1 Chi, some 2+.
Dark Force+
Press the same-strength P + K to trigger your character’s install (mirror clone, missile mode, Kitty helper…). Costs Chi.
Energy Bats (the match)
One round: deplete the foe’s health to take a bat; unhit health slowly recovers. Take all their bats (2 by default) to win.

Strengths & weaknessescontents ↑

B.B. Hood · Trap zoner

Strengths

  • Premier zoner — high, low, and air missiles plus traps lock the screen down.
  • Cool Hunting does huge damage and chip.
  • Sneaky low/overhead mix-ups (Malice & Mine, crawl) and a double jump.

Weaknesses

  • Weak up close once you’re past the missiles.
  • Several specials are slow / punishable on block.
  • Leans on spacing — gets opened up if cornered.

Strategy & game plancontents ↑

Build the wall
Alternate Smile & Missile (ground, high/low) and Happy & Missile (air) to fence the opponent out; walk behind them.
Mix the openings
Once they respect the missiles, sneak Malice & Mine (low) and the crawl, then convert into a chain.
Cash it in
Confirm any chain into Cool Hunting, or end in Sentimental Typhoon → ES Pursuit in the corner for max damage.
