How to read these comboscontents ↑

Combos read left → right; a faint › separates steps, italics are notes (launch, SJ, OTG, XX, dash). Normals are coloured buttons that match your Thor face buttons; a named pill like a special move means “do that move” (hover it for the motion). Difficulty is ●○○–●●●; core marks the staples carried over from the beginner guide.

MvC2 has only four attack buttons — two punches (LP/HP) and two kicks (LK/HK) — plus two assists. There is no medium attack like in Street Fighter, so old 6-button FAQ notation (jab/strong/fierce) is folded onto these four here.

Motions are written relative to your facing — F = toward the opponent, B = away — so they read correctly on both sides of the screen.

Combo vocabulary & the mechanics they use

Everything the routes below rely on. (The full command list & all team/system mechanics live in the sibling beginner guide, mvc2-cammy-bbhood-cyclops.html.)

Magic series
The ground/air chain. Press attacks weak→strong — LP → LK → HP → HK — each link cancels into the next. Tapping a light twice climbs the series.
Launcher
A HK or D/DF+HP hit that pops the foe up; you auto-super-jump after to chase them — that is an Aerial Rave.
Super jump (SJ)
Flick D→U to leap high after a launch.
Double jump (dj)
Press U again in the air. Only some characters (Cyclops, BB Hood) have it.
XX — cancel
Interrupt a move’s recovery into a special or super, e.g. a chain XX Cyclone Kick.
Super-cancel
Cancel a special into a super (e.g. basket XX Cool Hunting).
OTG
“Off the ground.” Some moves hit a downed foe — letting you OTG-relaunch for more.
DHC
Delayed Hyper Combo — cancel one character’s super into a partner’s super for team damage.
Charge
[B]F = hold back ~1s then press forward+button. [D]U = hold down, then up.
mash
Tap the button rapidly to add hits (Gene Splice, Charging Punches, Reverse Shaft Breaker).
Assist / snapback
A1/A2 call a partner. QCF+A (1 bar) snaps the foe’s point character out.

Cammy · Rushdown point

Ground BnB — meterless
CA-01Bread & butter●○○ core
XX › Cannon Drill
The first thing to learn. Double-jab into fierce, cancel the fierce into the drill.
CA-02Low BnB (OTG-able)●○○
cr. XX › Cannon Drill
All lows — opens crouchers. The drill knocks down so you can OTG after.
CA-03Range BnB●●○
› B+ XX › Cannon Drill
B+HK has extra reach (you can swap it for standing HP). Great after a poke.
CA-04Knockdown ender●○○
XX › Cannon Spike
Same start, Cannon Spike ender for a hard knockdown / anti-air finish.
Hit-confirm into Super (1 bar)
CA-05Tap-tap super●○○ core
XX › Spin Drive Smasher
The BnB, but end in the super. ~12 hits. Your everyday 1-bar punish.
CA-06Low into super●●○
cr. XX › Spin Drive Smasher
~9 hits. Not every hit of the super connects from lows, but it stays an OTG threat.
CA-07Jump-in into super●●○ core

Jump-in adds 2 hits (~14). Confirm the jab string before committing the bar.
CA-08Dash low → vertical super●●○
dash cr. › (pause) Reverse Shaft Breaker
Pause a tick after the knockdown HK so the rising super catches them.
CA-09Cross-up confirm●●○

The cross-up only confuses — confirm off the cr.LK before the super.
CA-10Knuckle into super●●●

Cancel after just the FIRST hit of the knuckle or the 2nd hit knocks them away.
CA-11Easy corner OTG super●●●

In the corner the super leaves them falling in place — her easiest OTG into a rave.
CA-12Super → OTG → air●●●

Spin Drive Smasher is OTG-able: dash, scoop with cr.LK and fly.

2 hits — for when you are not comfortable air-dashing the rave yet.

Throws & resets
CA-22Tick-throw → air●●○
› tick F+ throw › dash cr. OTG › cr. › SJ

CA-23Throw loop (corner)●●●
D+ launch › SJ › land F+ throw › cr. OTG › cr. › SJ › repeat…
Chain the rave slowly so you land in throw range, suplex, OTG, repeat.
CA-24Counter bait●●○
on block Cannon Revenge counter
After a blocked jump-in, the counter snags their mashed poke. Do not overuse — it is readable.
