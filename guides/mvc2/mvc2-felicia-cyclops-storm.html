How MvC2 works — controls & team mechanicscontents ↑

Everything you need to play, beyond these three characters’ own moves. MvC2 is 3-on-3 tag: one fights, two assist.

Movement

Tap toward the foe twice (or press both punches). Back-back dashes away.
Super jumpDU
Flick down→up to leap high — this is how you chase a launched foe for an air combo.
Air mobility
Storm has an 8-way air-dash, flight and a tri-jump; Felicia & Cyclops rely on normal + super jumps (Cyclops has a double jump).
Defence
Block / air-guardB / DB
Hold back (high) or down-back (low). You can air-block air attacks/projectiles/supers — but not a grounded normal.
Advancing Guard (push-block) blocking
Press both punches as you block to shove the attacker off — your #1 escape from pressure.
Variable CounterB→DB→D+
Tag a partner in with a counter-attack while blocking — 1 bar.
Tech roll
Tap toward/away as you land from a knockdown to roll and avoid an OTG.
Offence
Magic series
The chain: press attacks weak→strong (LP → LK → HP → HK); each cancels into the next. Tapping a light twice climbs the series.
Launcher → Aerial Rave
A launching normal (often HK / DF+HP) pops them up; super-jump after to chase with an air chain.
OTG
“Off the ground” — some moves hit a downed foe, letting you relaunch (Felicia & Cyclops live on this).
Throws / snapbackF
Close f/b + HP or HK throws. QCF + assist (1 bar) snaps the foe’s point character out.
Team mechanics
Variable Assist /
Press A1 / A2 to call a partner’s assist move (set by their α/β/γ type). The backbone of the team.
Variable Attack / tag
Tag a partner in (they enter with an attack). Order: who’s in front fights; the rest assist.
Variable Combination
Both/all on-screen supers at once (costs a bar each).
Delayed Hyper Combo (DHC)
Cancel one character’s super into a partner’s super — the team’s damage + the safe way to bring a fresh character in.
Super meter
Fills as you fight (up to 5 bars). Supers cost 1; spend on DHCs, snapbacks, or big single supers.

How to play this teamcontents ↑

Why this team (in plain terms)

A fast rushdown point, an anchor whose assist makes that rushdown safe, and a top-tier closer who can also run away and win on her own:

  • Felicia is your point — small, fast, and built to get in, open people up and cash out with her insane OTG combos. Her catch: she’s frail and has no real zoning, so she needs help getting in.
  • Cyclops is your glue — his β Gene Splice is one of the best anti-air assists in the game, so it covers Felicia’s approach; his Mega Optic Blast is a perfect DHC relay into Storm.
  • Storm is your anchor — the game’s best battery target, a huge Lightning Storm for damage, and Hail Storm, the safest DHC in the game. Even down to her alone she can runaway and steal games.

The glue is anti-air: Gene Splice makes Felicia’s jump-ins and dash-ins safe, Felicia and Cyclops feed meter to Storm, and Storm’s safe DHC + runaway mean a dropped character isn’t the end.

Team order: Felicia → Cyclops → Storm. Assists: Felicia α (Delta Kick), Cyclops β (Gene Splice), Storm α (Whirlwind).

Your first-day game plan

  • Play Felicia and get in — dash, super-jump, or roll forward with Rolling Buckler. Touch them and do › Sand Splash.
  • Call Cyclops () before you approach — Gene Splice covers you if they jump or mash.
  • Block when it isn’t your turn (hold back / down-back).
  • With a bar, end a chain in Dancing Flash — or DHC it forward for the kill.

Learning path — what to practicecontents ↑

  • 1. Felicia’s touch — Land cr.LK/LP chains into Sand Splash, and always call the Gene Splice assist before you commit to getting in.
  • 2. Launch → air → OTG — Launch (HK), air-rave into Delta Kick, then OTG-relaunch with cr.HK or the Rolling Buckler → Sliding Kick. This is most of Felicia’s damage.
  • 3. Cyclops basics — cr.LK/LP, st.HK XX Cyclone Kick / Mega Optic Blast — and use Gene Splice as your on-point anti-air when Felicia is hurt.
  • 4. Storm in the air — Launch → air-rave XX Lightning Attack XX Lightning Storm. Learn to confirm into it from anywhere.
  • 5. The DHC kill — Felicia super → Cyclops Mega Optic Blast → Storm Lightning/Hail Storm. Bank meter with Felicia/Cyclops, cash it on Storm.
  • 6. Storm as anchor — Runaway (super-jump, air-dash, typhoons) to close games; tri-jump cross-ups when you want to rush. Advanced but match-winning.
